Welcome to Byte Archives

This is a one man effort (me: Spiro Floropoulos) to provide a centralized platform where C programmers of all levels can access concise, high-quality information on various aspects of the C language.
Hopefully I will have a variety of resources here related to things you won't generally find in other places. I will be scouring discord, X, reddit, streams, past videos, github, etc, and pulling information from key individuals to create content you won't find anywhere else. Some of the people I will be pulling information from are:
  • Martins Mozeiko
  • Casey Muratori
  • Jonathan Blow
  • Ginger Bill
  • and more

What you'll find here

  • Expert insights and tips on C programming
  • Best practices and common pitfalls to avoid
  • Deep dives into complex C concepts
  • Practical examples and code snippets
